Why Futsal?

Futsal develops technique, quick thinking, and close control faster than any other format of the game. The smaller pitch, heavier ball, and constant involvement mean every player touches the ball up to 600% more than in a typical 11-a-side match.

At North London Hawks, we've been running futsal sessions since our founding in 2021. Our coaches bring international-level experience from Hungary, the National Futsal League, and Maccabi GB -- creating a programme that's serious about development but never loses sight of enjoyment.

Our Coaches

A team with real experience at the highest levels of the game.

Tamas

Head Coach · UEFA B Futsal

Tamas has been involved with futsal since his youth football days in Hungary. He holds a UEFA B qualification in futsal and a BA in sports coaching and education. He's worked with players across all age groups and founded North London Hawks in 2021.

Jordan

Coach · Level 2 Futsal · GK Specialist

Jordan has been involved in futsal for over 10 years, playing in the National League and coaching both youth and women's futsal. He holds a Level 2 futsal qualification and specialises in goalkeeper development.
