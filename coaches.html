Meet the Coaches

Qualified, experienced coaches who know what it takes to develop young players.

Jordan

Coach · Level 2 Futsal · GK Specialist

Jordan has been involved in futsal for over 10 years, playing in the National League and coaching both youth and women's futsal. He holds a Level 2 futsal qualification and specialises in goalkeeper development. His approach combines technical drills with match-realistic scenarios, making sure every player gets the intensity and the coaching they need to progress.

Tamas

Head Coach · UEFA B Futsal

Tamas has been involved with futsal since his youth football days in Hungary. He holds a UEFA B qualification in futsal and a BA in sports coaching and education. He's worked with players across all age groups and founded North London Hawks in 2021. His coaching philosophy centres on building technically complete players who can think faster and play smarter than the opposition.
